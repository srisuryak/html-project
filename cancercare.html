What is cancer

A disease caused by an uninhibited growth of abnormal cella in any part of the body

How can cancer can be cured ?

there are three ways of curing, restricting and managing cancerous cells.

They are given below

1-medical oncology:Cancer is treated through chemotherapy. Chemotherapy is a drug treatment that uses powerful chemicals to kill the fast growing cancerous cells. It is the most commonly used way of treatment.

2--Radiation Oncology: Radiation therapy is the use of precisely calculated doses of high energy X-rays to the affected areas. It is a pain free treatment.

3-Surgical Oncology:Surgical management of cancer has evolved in many ways. While surgery alone is enough for early cancers, most advanced cancers require a multimodality treatment which includes managing the cancer with surgery, radiotherapy and chemotherapy
